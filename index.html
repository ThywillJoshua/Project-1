Lions

The African Lion

The lion is a species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus
Panthera. It has a muscular, deep-chested body, short, rounded head,
round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
It is sexually dimorphic; adult male lions have a prominent mane.
